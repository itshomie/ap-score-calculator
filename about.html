About APScoreCalculator.app

APScoreCalculator.app is an independent study tool for AP® students, parents, tutors, and teachers. The goal is simple: make it easier to turn practice-test raw scores into a practical score estimate without signing up for an account or digging through a cluttered page.

The calculator is designed for planning, not official reporting. Official AP® scores are produced by College Board scoring processes, and college credit policies are set by individual institutions. This site helps students understand whether their practice performance appears closer to a 3, 4, or 5 range so they can make better study decisions.

APScoreCalculator.app is not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by College Board. We do not use College Board logos, and we link to College Board resources only as plain-text references for users who want official background.

Contact

Editorial Standard

The calculator is designed to be useful, transparent, and easy to understand. Estimates are based on section weights and approximate score bands, not confidential official cut scores. If a source, score format, or subject preset appears incorrect, send the details by email.
