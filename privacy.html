Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026.

APScoreCalculator.app is built as a simple browser-based study tool. The calculator does not require an account and does not ask for a student name, school, College Board account, or email address to estimate a score.

Calculator Inputs

Raw scores entered into the practice estimator are processed in the browser for the purpose of showing an estimate. The current version does not store those score entries on a server.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand aggregate site usage, such as page views, device type, approximate location, and how visitors move through the site. Google Analytics may use cookies or similar identifiers to collect usage data. We use this information to improve the calculator, subject pages, accessibility, and site performance.

Advertising and Cookies

If we display Google AdSense or other advertising, third-party vendors may use cookies, web beacons, IP addresses, or similar identifiers to serve, measure, limit fraud, and personalize ads. Those third parties may place and read cookies in your browser or use web beacons and IP addresses as part of ad serving. Google explains how it uses data from partner sites at How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Where consent is required, personalized advertising should not be served unless the appropriate consent has been collected.

Contact Email

If you contact us at support [at] apscorecalculator.app, we will receive the information you choose to include in your email and use it to respond to your request.

Children's Privacy

This site is intended as a general study utility and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has sent us personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

Independent Site

APScoreCalculator.app is not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by College Board.
